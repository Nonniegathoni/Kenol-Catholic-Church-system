Youth Activities

Charitable Visits

Annually, the youth of our church embark on a heartwarming charitable journey, reaching out to the less fortunate in our community. Typically scheduled during the holiday season, these visits involve distributing essentials, organizing food drives, and spending quality time with those in need. It's a powerful reminder of the joy that comes from giving back and fostering a sense of community.

MUSIC FESTIVALS

Celebrating the joy of music and worship, the youth organizes an annual music festival that showcases the talents within the church community. Held during the fall season, this vibrant event features performances by the church choir, soloists, and instrumentalists. It's an opportunity for the youth to express their devotion through music and share their artistic gifts with the congregation.

NIGHT VIGILS

The youth actively engages in spiritual growth through annual virgil masses, which are held on significant occasions in the church calendar, such as Easter and Christmas Eve. These overnight prayer sessions create a serene atmosphere for reflection, worship, and a deepening of their faith. It's a time for spiritual renewal and a unique bonding experience for the youth community.
